How Families Can Support Learning

Cybersecurity does not need to feel complicated. Real situations and repeatable sentences help children remember.

Set Rules Together

For example: do not share passwords, ask an adult before opening unknown links, and share less account information.

Practise One Chinese Sentence

“I cannot share my password.” “I should ask my teacher.” “This is safer.”

Watch, Then Discuss

After a video or story, ask: What was unsafe? What would you do?
